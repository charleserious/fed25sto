LEGO PRIMO is for babies! LEGO PRIMO toys offer a range of fun play, learning and challenging possibilities for children 0-18 months old. LEGO PRIMO encourages the child to experiment, learn and develop. LEGO DUPLO toys are for preschoolers. It is a series of fun toys that will stimulate the senses and motor skills of the child. LEGO DUPLO turns playing into learning. The LEGO SYSTEM range consists of hundreds of sets for creative children that like using their imagination. The basic idea is this: Build a great idea, take it apart when you get a new one, and build something entirely different. The sets are made for different ages - but you can combine them all. LEGO TECHNIC sets are for children who enjoy constructing exciting and realistic models with real life features such as motors, gears, pneumatic, fiber optic system, remote control, control center and barcode pilot for programming. Try the new LEGO Dacta WWW site! The LEGO DACTA range has been specially developed to support group activities in early years and aid learning in schools. This educational range is curriculum relevant and available via educational distributors.
